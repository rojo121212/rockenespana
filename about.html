About Me

Hello! My name is Rocky, and I am passionate about learning, exploring, and creating during my time here in Spain.

I am pursuing a dual major in Data Science/Computer Science and Literature. While those are my primary academic interests, I love learning about many different subjects and finding ways to connect technology, creativity, and service to others.

My dream is to use technology and hands-on work to make a positive impact on people's lives. That goal is a big part of why I chose to participate in my internship here in Sevilla.

Why I Made This Website

This website was created to document my experiences in Spain and share them with friends, family, and loved ones back home.

Throughout the summer, I'll be posting updates about my travels, cultural experiences, internship work, photos, and everyday adventures.

My Background

I enjoy programming, data analysis, problem solving, writing, and exploring new ideas. I am always looking for opportunities to learn something new and challenge myself.
